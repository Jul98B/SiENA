## available stats to export to csv
charge			charge of storages (ev, bat, tank, ...)
configs			device configurations
consumption		consumption of all devices
counters		activation counters, ...
grouped			consumption of device types (base, movable, ...)
heat			heat generation, demand of households, ...
msgs			number of messages needed for communication in interval
pcharge			charge in %
quality			dsm quality
state			states of all devices
stats			stats displayed at end of simulation
time			time needed for communication in interval

## citygrid
rounds			number of rounds in interval

powermatcher	price

web				exports stats for online simulator
